SMS notifications from Salt Cubed Inc.

Salt Cubed Inc. operates a transactional SMS notification service for internal business operations, including scheduling reminders, workflow updates, and time-sensitive operational alerts. This page describes the messaging program and how consent is established.

What you'll receive

  • Scheduling reminders and appointment confirmations
  • Workflow and project status updates
  • Time-sensitive operational alerts
  • Internal business communications

How consent works

  • This service is used solely by the authorized Salt Cubed Inc. account owner.
  • Consent is established directly by the account owner, who initiates messaging by texting the dedicated number from their own mobile device.
  • No messages are sent to third parties, and no contact information is collected from anyone other than the account owner.
Messages are sent from: Salt Cubed Inc. via a dedicated long code number.

Message frequency: Varies based on operational activity. You may receive up to 10 messages per month.

To opt out: Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe immediately. Reply HELP for assistance.

Message and data rates may apply. This service is provided by Salt Cubed Inc. for internal operational communications only. Your information will not be shared with third parties for marketing purposes. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Privacy Policy

Effective: June 18, 2026

Who We Are

Salt Cubed Inc. ("Salt Cubed," "we," "us") is a California-based production and entertainment services company. This policy describes how we collect and use information in connection with our SMS notification service.

Information We Collect

When you subscribe to SMS notifications, we collect your name and mobile phone number. We use this information solely to deliver the transactional SMS messages you have requested.

How We Use Your Information

Your mobile number is used exclusively to send you operational and transactional text messages, including scheduling reminders, workflow updates, and time-sensitive alerts related to Salt Cubed business operations. We do not use your information for marketing or advertising purposes.

SMS Messaging

By subscribing, you agree to receive SMS messages from Salt Cubed Inc. Message frequency varies based on operational activity, up to 10 messages per month. Message and data rates may apply. To opt out at any time, reply STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share information with service providers (including Twilio, our SMS delivery provider) solely as necessary to deliver the service. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.

Data Retention

We retain your contact information for as long as you remain subscribed to SMS notifications. Upon opt-out, your information is removed from our active messaging lists within 10 business days.

Your Rights

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP. To request deletion of your information, contact us at the address below.

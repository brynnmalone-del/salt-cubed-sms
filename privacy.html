Privacy Policy

Effective: June 18, 2026

Who We Are

Salt Cubed Inc. ("Salt Cubed," "we," "us") is a California-based production and entertainment services company. This policy describes how we collect and use information in connection with our SMS notification service.

Information We Collect

This SMS notification service is used solely by the authorized Salt Cubed Inc. account owner. We do not collect names or phone numbers from the public through this website. The only mobile number involved is the account owner's own number, which they provide when configuring the messaging service.

How We Use Your Information

The account owner's mobile number is used exclusively to send operational and transactional text messages, including scheduling reminders, workflow updates, and time-sensitive alerts related to Salt Cubed business operations. We do not use this information for marketing or advertising purposes.

SMS Messaging

Consent to receive SMS messages from Salt Cubed Inc. is established directly by the account owner, who initiates messaging by texting the dedicated number from their own mobile device. Message frequency varies based on operational activity, up to 10 messages per month. Message and data rates may apply. To opt out at any time, reply STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share information with service providers (including Twilio, our SMS delivery provider) solely as necessary to deliver the service. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.

Data Retention

We retain the account owner's contact information for as long as the messaging service remains active. Upon opt-out, the information is removed from our active messaging lists within 10 business days.

Your Rights

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP. To request deletion of your information, contact us at the address below.
